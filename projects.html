Importance of Mentorship

A good example to emphases mentorship is find in John8:12 I am the light of the world: he that followeth me shall not walk in darkness, but shall have the light of life.

Without mentorship, you spend productive hours finding out things for yourself that could take just a simple step if guarded by someone who has passed through the process.

Being a beneficiary of mentorship, I would like to devote some of my time to providing mentorship to the younger generation. Consequently, I have decided to journal some of my journey for the purpose.

I will be putting this out in the social media for a wider reach. I will also connect with religious and community centers to develop a program in their activities to accommodate my purpose.

Our youths really need guidance as there is so much distraction and information overload, peer-pressure leading to wrong decisions.
