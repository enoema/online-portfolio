Our Goal

The goal of this website is to help the young become self reliant people by introducing them early to programming!

Young people are like the growing plant that needs the right condition to grow into fruitful trees. The growing plant needs:

  1. Sunlight
  2. Water
  3. Environment
  4. Nutrients
  5. Tendering
Our society is becoming more complex with so much distractions and negative influence from social media and peers. If our young people are not properly modelled and their attention welll directed, they will definitely become unproductive and unfruitful- a bleak future awaits us. We want to avoid such a negative force. The future we talk about is not a distant time but just few years from now.The work must earnestly began with commitment and sacrifice on all fronts. The parents, teachers, media and the community.
